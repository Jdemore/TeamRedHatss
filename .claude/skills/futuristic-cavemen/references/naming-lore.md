# Naming and Lore

## Naming Rules

1. All player-facing text uses **Hiragana** (see hiragana-japanese skill).
2. Every Hiragana string has a **romaji transliteration** in code comments.
3. Internal code uses **English** PascalCase identifiers.
4. Asset file names use **English** kebab-case (e.g., `bone-club-prefab.prefab`).

Pattern: `// UI: ほらあな の おと (horaana no oto - "Sounds of the Cave")`

---

## World Lore

### The Ancient Power (いにしえ の ちから / inishie no chikara)

Long before recorded history, a mysterious energy pulsed through the earth. It flowed along crystal veins deep underground, surfacing at places of great geological power: volcanoes, mountain peaks, ocean trenches, and deep caverns.

The first tribe to discover this energy found that it responded to **rhythm**. Beating drums in specific patterns could channel the power -- calming wild dinosaurs, growing crystal formations, lighting dark caves, and forging impossibly advanced tools from raw stone and bone.

This tribe became the **Drummers of the Deep** (ふかき の うちて / fukaki no uchite). They built a civilization around rhythm, where every important act -- hunting, building, healing, celebrating -- was performed to a beat.

### The Great Silence (おおいなる しじま / ooinaru shijima)

One day, the rhythm stopped. The ancient power went dormant. Crystals dimmed. Dinosaurs grew restless. The tribe's technology failed.

Now, a new Drummer has risen. By playing the ancient songs and rediscovering the lost rhythms, they must reawaken the power, calm the dinosaurs, and restore the world.

This is the player's journey.
